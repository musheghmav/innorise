Why to work with us ?

There might be million companies doing software development, why Innorise ?

  • BEST IN CLASS DEVELOPMENT

    In today's world, software products must completely satisfy their users. Still, development speed and applied creativity remain critical priorities. If you’re looking for software development services, Innorise is here to create innovative, engaging products for you, while at the same time ensuring rapid time to market. We develop your application in the most flexible and extendible way, conforming to the best coding standards and practices.

  • WE CARE ABOUT YOUR PRODUCT

    We care deeply about every app we develop acting as a co-founder with no equity. Innorise’s approach is to develop your app and be proud of its success. For this reason we will share all our knowledge with you to make sure your app conforms to all the best practices and standards. We also assist you in finding cost effective solutions for maintaining and delivering your app to the market.

  • CUTTING EDGE DESIGNS

    An app is only as good as its user experience. This depends not only on backend but also on its design. We have a team of sophisticated and carefully selected graphic designers and animators who secure the most state of the art design with the best user experience for your app. All our designs are crystal clear, sharp, modern and sophisticated – in line with our general design philosophy.

  • LIGHTNING FAST DEVELOPMENT

    We understand how challenging it is for startups to deliver their apps to the market on time. Innorise’s intellectual potential is huge. Our engineers are extremely skilled in multiple fields of software development. We are experienced in dealing with complex technical challenges and pressing deadlines and we know how to resolve these challenges on time. Our young team of enthusiasts love challenges and we won’t rest until we find the right solution for the problem, ensuring that the development will be done as fast as possible.

  • FLEXIBLE FEES

    We offer flexible pricing options. You can choose between project or resource and material based business models - whatever best suits your needs and preferences. We offer extremely competitive prices in the outsource software development area. Still, you get a software both well written and documented, ideally suited to maintain and extend your app with ease in the future. Our prices are customised for the greatest business value and we charge nothing for any required changes.

  • FREE ONE YEAR SUPPORT

    We guarantee software development in accordance with best coding standards and practices. Moreover, our quality assurance team makes sure that your app is always running smoothly. Still, in real life scenarios unanticipated issues may occur - we take this very seriously. Innorise offers free one year support, including bug fixes after development. Even modest improvements that help your app succeed are considered within the scope of our free support service.

  • INSTANT 24/7 COMMUNICATION

    We are extremely customer-focused in assisting our clients 24/7. We will be happy to assist you with all types of questions/issues in any mode of communication, be it WhatsApp, Skype or regular cellular/landline call.

  • MADE IN ARMENIA

    Armenia was once the Silicon Valley of the former Soviet Union. Forty percent of the mainframe computers for the Soviet military were designed here. Indeed, the USSR’s most crucial scientific, R&D, and manufacturing activities in segments like mainframe and industrial computing, electronics, semiconductors, software development and more were greatly dependent on Armenian innovators and manufacturers.
